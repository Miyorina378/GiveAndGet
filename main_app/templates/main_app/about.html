About

GiveAndGet is a web application for buying, selling, or trading anything you want.



Our web application is aimed at users who have items that they are no longer using but don't want to throw them away as trash. They can sell or exchange them for something else they need.

This way, a user can buy or trade an item that is no longer available, such as limited items.
